Drake's Rise to Fame

Drake's real name is Aubrey Drake Graham. He was born in Toronto, Canada in 1986. Unlike a lot of rappers, Drake has been consistent with his music style and personal style which many people appreciate. He is known for blending hip-hop and R&B music.

Learn more about Drake on his official website: Drake Official Site

Drake's Biggest Accomplishments

Drake's music style is consistent and emotional. He uses a lot of rhyming which makes his songs very catchy. His success has made him one of the most influential artists in modern music and his lyrics often feel very personal.
